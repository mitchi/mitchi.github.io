Feather Fall

Fall without taking damage

Transmutation
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: See text
Spell Lists: Alchemy,Arcane,Divine,Nature,Psychic
Levels: 1,3
Cast range: close

A shimmering, weightless aura envelops targets, transforming deadly plunges into graceful descents. The spellâ€™s duration and secondary effects grow more potent when cast at higher levels.

Level 1 Feather Fall

Duration: 1 minute/level
Number of Creatures Affected: 1 creature/level
Effect:

  • Targets fall at a harmless rate (60 ft./round), take no damage, and land on their feet.
  • The spell activates retroactively when a fall begins and persists for its duration, protecting against repeated falls (e.g., collapsing bridges, mid-air combat).
  • Creatures can still act normally while falling (e.g., attack, cast spells).

Level 3 Feather Fall

Duration: 1 hour/level
Number of Creatures Affected: 1 creature/level
Effect:

  • All Level 1 benefits, plus:
  • Glide Control: Targets steer their descent horizontally, moving up to half the vertical fall distance laterally (e.g., fall 60 ft. down, glide 30 ft. sideways).
