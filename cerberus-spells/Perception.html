Perception

Divination
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: See text
Spell Lists: Alchemy,Arcane,Divine,Nature,Psychic
Levels: 1,3,5,7,9,10

The Perception spell is a powerful divination that dramatically enhances the caster's or a willing creature's ability to detect the hidden and notice the obscure. This magic goes beyond merely sharpening the five senses; it creates a mystical awareness that allows the target to perceive the world with supernatural acuity.

When cast, the beneficiary's senses heighten progressively. Colors become more vivid, sounds clearer, and scents more distinct. But the spell's effects extend far beyond these physical enhancements. The target develops an almost preternatural awareness of their surroundings, able to notice minute details and sense things that would normally be imperceptible.

This heightened perception grants a considerable advantage in detecting hidden objects, creatures, and even magical effects. The spell's beneficiary sees their Perception score increase significantly, allowing them to spot well-concealed traps and notice stealthy creatures Moreover, when a creature under the effect of Perception succeeds in seeing a Stealth creature, it gets a 50% bonus to its Reflex score against the first action of that creature.

The spell also befits a special skill called Awareness to its target. Awareness allows the creature to automatically see invisible creatures or creatures hiding in darkness (no check needed).

The Perception spell can be improved by metamagic abilities, such as Repeat Spell, which allows the caster to target multiple creatures for the buff.
