Cleaning

Clean, wash an area. The spell even sharpens knives!

Transmutation
Action: Full
Components: V,S,M (a small scrub brush)
Duration: 1 hour
Spell Lists: Alchemy,Arcane,Divine,Nature,Psychic
Levels: 1

The Cleaning spell is a versatile transmutation that magically tidies, sanitizes, and performs minor repairs in areas such as living rooms and kitchens. When cast, the spell works over the course of one hour, creating a mesmerizing spectacle of moving objects and self-cleaning items. The caster must focus on their desired end result for the cleaning process, and the spell intelligently arranges items based on this mental image.

Objects within the area of effect are thoroughly cleaned and organized according to the caster's intention. The spell also identifies and highlights magical or mundane contaminants, including common toxins, which glow faintly in a way visible only to the caster. However, it's important to note that the spell does not detect or remove major poisons or toxins.

One of the spell's key features is its selective cleaning capability, allowing the caster to exclude specific objects or areas from the cleaning effect at the time of casting. This can be particularly useful in preserving certain arrangements or protecting sensitive items.

In addition to its primary cleaning function, the spell performs several other useful tasks. It can carry out minor repairs, such as mending small tears or gluing broken ceramics. The spell also purifies water and removes common, minor toxins from food, making it valuable for food preparation and safety. As an added benefit, all bladed utensils in the affected area are automatically sharpened and honed.

Despite its versatility, the Cleaning spell has limitations. It cannot repair significant damage, remove curses, or neutralize powerful poisons or toxins. The spell is primarily designed for domestic and commercial use, such as maintaining cleanliness in homes, inns, restaurants, or shops. It's also excellent for post-event cleanup or carefully cleaning delicate artifacts.
