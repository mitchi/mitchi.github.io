Epic Levitate

This spell was crafted exclusively for the purpose of levitating entire cities into the skies

Transmutation
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: Permanent
Spell Lists: Arcane
Levels: 10
Range: far
Radius: 10 kilometers per caster level

Epic Levitate is a spell of remarkable ingenuity, creating a perpetual cushion of levitation magic beneath objects or structures. This spell is designed to use the minimal amount of magical energy necessary to maintain a state of floatation, ensuring efficiency in its operation. Despite this economical allocation of magic, the spell possesses a dynamic adaptability; in the event of an impact, it can instantaneously redistribute and intensify the levitation magic to form additional layers of cushioning. This rapid response mechanism is so robust that even the weight of Colossal creatures cannot overwhelm it.

The most striking feature of Epic Levitate is its ability to mimic the sensation of solid ground. Structures supported by this spell don't just float; they maintain a stability and firmness that belie their airborne nature. This unique combination of minimal magic usage, responsive reinforcement, and the creation of a ground-like experience makes Epic Levitate a spell of both subtle finesse and formidable power.
