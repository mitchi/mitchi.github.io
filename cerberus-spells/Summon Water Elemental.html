Summon Water Elemental

Summon a powerful water elemental to fight at your side

Conjuration
Damage type: Physical
Action: Full
Components: V,S,M
Duration: minutes/level
Spell Lists: Arcane
Levels: 2,4,6,8,10
Origin: Archon

If a suitable body of water is available, Summon Water Elemental is a Transmutation spell, and it is quicker, as it now takes two ticks to cast. Otherwise, it is a Conjuration spell and takes 3 ticks to cast. The Water Elemental has the same Reflex Save as its original spellcaster. A Water Elemental moves three times faster in water.

Special Abilities

  • Multishot. The Water Elemental can attack one additional target. Additional spell levels add an additional target. Unlocked at Level 2.
  • Vapor Fog. The Water Elemental creates a vapor fog in a radius around it, obstructing all vision to 5 feet and decreasing perception checks by 10. Unlocked at level 4
  • Water Mail. Returns 50% of melee damage taken. Radius is 10 ft per caster level. Unlocked at level 6
  • Hydrokinesis. Works like the equivalent lower-level Telekinesis, but only with water. In this case, Hydrokinesis would work like a level 6 Telekinesis spell. Unlocked at level 8
  • Absorb Hostile Water. Absorb the water in surrounding creatures, slowly killing them. The radius is 20ft per caster level. Fortitude Save to resist or lose 10% of max HP. If the Fortitude Save works, lose 5% instead. The stolen HP heals the Water Elemental. Unlocked at level 10.
