Tempest Gale

A devastating wind attack

Evocation
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: Permanent
Spell Lists: Alchemy, Arcane, Divine, Nature, Psychic,
Levels: 1,3,5,7,9,10
Range: medium

Tempest Gale is a potent wind elemental spell that summons forth a tremendous gust of wind, hurling it with immense force towards one or more targets. Unlike sustained wind effects, Tempest Gale manifests as a short-lived but incredibly powerful burst of air.

As the spell increases in level, it demonstrates the ability to affect multiple targets simultaneously, making it a formidable force in combat against groups of foes. The spellcaster has significant control over the direction and force of the wind, allowing them to move creatures and objects roughly in the desired direction.

When Tempest Gale affects a creature, the wind doesn't just push them - it also cuts them as if they were attacked by thousands of knife-like air currents, adding a slashing damage component to the spell's effects. This makes Tempest Gale not just a tool for repositioning enemies, but also a potent damage-dealing spell.
