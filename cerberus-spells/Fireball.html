Fireball

Known as the most destructive elemental magic spell

Evocation
Damage type: Fire
Action: Standard
Components: V,S
Duration: Instantaneous
Spell Lists: Arcane
Levels: 3,5,7,9,10
Range: far

Fireball is a foundational elemental spell, renowned for its devastating potential and versatility in combat. As one of the most destructive spells in a caster's arsenal, it combines raw power with tactical flexibility.

The caster conjures a compact sphere of flame, imbuing it with arcane energy before launching it towards the intended target. As the fireball hurtles through the air, it rapidly expands, reaching its full size upon impact. The size and power of the fireball are directly proportional to the caster's skill and the level at which the spell is cast.

When multiple fireballs are directed at the same target, their destructive force typically does not compound. However, when cast at the epic level of spellcasting (level 10), the damage from two fireballs does stack, resulting in cataclysmic destruction.

Size Categories

  • Medium (Level 3): Affects an area comparable to a small room (20ft radius)
  • Large (Level 5): Engulfs an area the size of a large chamber (40ft radius)
  • Huge (Level 7): Consumes an area equivalent to a small building (60ft radius)
  • Gargantuan (Level 9): Devastates an area the size of a city block (80ft radius)
  • Colossal (Level 10): Obliterates an area comparable to a small town district (160ft radius)
