Elemental Wall

Summon a towering barrier of pure elemental energy, its nature shifting between searing flames, crushing stone, howling winds, or other primal forces at the wielder's command

Conjuration
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: See text
Spell Lists: Arcane,Divine,Nature,Psychic
Levels: 2,4,6,8,10

Elemental Wall is a versatile spell that allows the caster to create a barrier of pure elemental energy. The wall can be shaped as desired within size constraints and imbued with various elemental properties. To cast this spell, the caster must know an elemental spell of a lower level than the Elemental Wall being cast. This elemental spell determines the wall's nature, such as using an acid spell for a Wall of Acid or a fire spell for a Wall of Fire.

Casting Elemental Wall consumes two spell slots: one for the Elemental Wall spell itself and another lower-level slot for the elemental spell powering the wall. For example, a level 6 Acid Wall using level 5 Acid Splash would consume both a level 6 and a level 5 spell slot. The elemental spell powering the wall must be at least one level lower than the Elemental Wall spell being cast.

Possible elemental walls include, but are not limited to: Wall of Stone, Wall of Water, Wall of Acid, Wall of Fire, Wall of Wind, Wall of Force, Wall of Thunder, and Wall of Poison. The wall's shape can be customized but must adhere to the size restrictions based on the spell level and caster level. The wall's effects and damage are determined by the elemental spell used to power it. The duration of the wall depends on the element used. Some walls, such as Wall of Stone, are very stable and the duration will be equal to the duration of the elemental spell. Otherwise, the duration specified in the spell scaling table will be used.

Size Categories

  • Medium (Level 1): Affects an area comparable to a small room (20ft radius)
  • Large (Level 3): Affects an area the size of a large chamber (40ft radius)
  • Huge (Level 5): Affects an area equivalent to a small building (60ft radius)
  • Gargantuan (Level 7): Affects an area the size of a city block (80ft radius)
  • Colossal (Level 9): Affects an area comparable to a small town district (160ft radius)
  • Colossal+ (Level 10): Affects an area comparable to a small town district (640ft radius)
