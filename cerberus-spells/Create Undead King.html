Create Undead King

Only an incredible power can create an incredible being

Wizard 10, Necromancy
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: living humanoid creature or corpse of a creature dead for no longer than 2 rounds

By focusing arcane power through an onyx gem, powerful negative energy is released. This spell brute forces the transformation into an undead lord by overflowing a corpse with negative energy. If cast on a living humanoid, the spell fails on a Fortitude save but the target still takes damage equivalent to a successful Finger of Death spell (10 damage per CL). If cast on a corpse, there is no saving throw nor spell resistance. Here are the possible transformations :

  • Lich
  • Vampire
  • Mummy Lord
  • Death Knight
