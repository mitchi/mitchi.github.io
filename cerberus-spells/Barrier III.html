Barrier III

A powerful, invisible magical barrier

Wizard 9, Abjuration
Action: Standard
Components: V,S
Duration: rounds/level
Origin: Stoneheart
Target: close creature

This spell erects a barrier of force that protects the target creature from sound, heat, physical attacks and most targeted spells. The barrier does not protect against negative energy, mental attacks and other non-targeted spells.

The barrier spells have the following statistics:

  • Barrier: 5 hp/CL and 5ft max radius
  • Barrier II: 15 hp/CL and 10ft max radius
  • Barrier III: 45 hp/CL and 20ft max radius

Note 1: Custom versions of the Barrier spell can be created by mixing in Barrier traits (Barrier sphere, Energy Barrier, Long Barrier, Necro Barrier are a few examples). These traits usually have tradeoffs. For instance, they can decrease a barrier's durability or increase the number of spell slots required to cast the spell.

Note 2: Multiple barriers can stack just like layers of armor, but this increases the difficulty of the barrier. The spellcaster can layer at most 1 barrier for every 5 Intelligence points (e.g. 2 barriers with +10 INT)

Note 3: A barrier spell can be scaled using the Overcast ability. For instance, a level 4 Overcast Barrier will have 7.5 (8 hp) per caster level.
