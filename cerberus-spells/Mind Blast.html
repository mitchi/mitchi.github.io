Mind Blast

A surge of psychic energy assaults the target's mind, leaving their mental defenses weakened

Evocation
Damage type: Psychic
Action: Standard (2 ticks)
Spell Lists: Psychic
Levels: 1,3,5,7,9,10

The caster projects a concentrated burst of psychic energy, targeting the mind of a creature within range. On a successful ranged touch attack, the psychic assault deals psychic damage and temporarily erodes the target's mental fortitude, lowering their Will Save for one round (3 ticks) If the caster reduces a creature's Will Save to 0 using Mind Blast, the target becomes mentally stunned for 1 round.

Mind Blast can be countered through the following methods:

  1. Dodging the attack
  2. Barrier spells (to protect against the touch attack)
  3. Using restorative magic (e.g. Restoration)
  4. Boosting back the stat that took a penalty.

Only the strongest Mind Blast debuff may be active at a time on a creature.
