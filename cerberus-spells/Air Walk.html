Air Walk

Walk, run, jump on air

Transmutation
Action: Standard
Components: V,S,M
Duration: See text
Spell Lists: Alchemy,Arcane,Divine,Nature,Psychic
Levels: 1,3,5
Range: close

Air Walk allows the caster or target to tread on air as if walking on solid ground. The spell simulates the experience of climbing a mountain, with a maximum ascent or descent angle of approximately 45 degrees.
