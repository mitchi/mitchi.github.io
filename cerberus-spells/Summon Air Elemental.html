Summon Air Elemental

The strongest elemental summon for physical damage

Conjuration
Damage type: Physical
Action: See text
Components: V,S,M
Duration: rounds/level
Spell Lists: Arcane,Divine,Nature
Levels: 2,4,6,8,10

The Summon Air Elemental spell allows you to call forth a powerful air elemental to assist you in combat and other endeavors. The elemental's abilities and strength increase with the caster's power, making it a versatile and formidable ally.

If cast in an area with strong air currents or winds, the spell becomes an Evocation spell, reducing the casting time to two ticks. Without such conditions, the spell functions as a Conjuration spell, taking three ticks to cast.

The Air Elemental shares the same Will Save as its caster and can fly at twice its normal movement speed, making it incredibly mobile and hard to pin down.

The caster can also produce multiple air elementals by opting to conjure weaker ones. If the caster selects a weaker level, they will conjure 2 air elementals, with a 50% chance of conjuring a third. If the caster opts to conjure elementals two levels below, they will conjure 4 of them, with a 50% chance of conjuring 2 more, and so on.

Special Abilities

  • Aerial Agility: Starting at level 2, the Air Elemental gains a +2 bonus to its Armor Class due to its incredible maneuverability. This bonus increases by 1 for every two levels thereafter.

  • Whirlwind: Unlocked at level 4, the Air Elemental can transform into a whirlwind once per day, lifting and throwing small to medium-sized objects and creatures within a 20-foot radius. Affected targets must make a Strength save or be thrown 1d6 x 10 feet and take 3d6 bludgeoning damage.

  • Wind Wall: At level 6, the Air Elemental can create a wall of swirling air as a bonus action. This wall is 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick. Ranged weapon attacks passing through the wall have disadvantage, and gaseous effects cannot pass through.

  • Lightning Spark: Available at level 8, the Air Elemental can channel the static electricity in the air to unleash a powerful lightning bolt. This ability deals 8d6 lightning damage to a single target within 60 feet, with a Dexterity save for half damage.

  • Eye of the Storm: At level 10, the Air Elemental can create a 30-foot radius of calm air around itself, negating all wind effects within. Allies within this area gain advantage on ranged attacks, while enemies have disadvantage. The elemental can maintain this effect for up to 1 minute per day.
