Fireball II

The favorite spell of Aldor the Infernal

Wizard 5, Evocation
Damage type: Fire
Action: Standard
Components: V,S
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: far

The mage summons a fireball before them, which is then swiftly hurled towards a designated area. As the mage creates the fireball initially, they fashion it in a smaller form, and it gradually expands as it speeds towards its ultimate destination.
