Lazy Wizard (Epic Class)

The lazy wizard is a free spirit. Lazy, innovative, creative, and non-conventional.

  • BAB: 3/4
  • Good Saves: Will, Reflex
  • Bad Save: Fortitude
  • Skill Ranks: 4 per level
  • Hit Dice: d8

Class Skills: Appraise (Int), Bluff (Cha), Craft (Int), Fly (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Profession (Wis), Spellcraft (Int), Use Magic Device (Cha), and Diplomacy (Cha).

Class Features

The Lazy Wizard has the same class features as the Wizard (school, arcane bond, etc.). He can also learn the same discoveries.

Arcane Talent (Ex)

  • Cast any spell spontaneously after using it only once
  • Add Charisma modifier to caster level
  • Gain bonus spells for all levels equal to Charisma modifier
  • Apply any metamagic feat spontaneously without increased casting time (uses higher level spell slot)
  • Maintain any spell for a number of rounds equal to Charisma modifier without concentration
  • Add Intelligence + Charisma modifiers as flat damage bonus to all spells

Arcane Combining (Ex)

Spell Absorption (Ex)

At level 10, absorb spell power from objects and incoming spells. As a ready action, absorb an incoming spell by beating it in an opposed caster level check (1d20 + CL > incoming spell's CL). Release absorbed spells later using your caster level and Intelligence for CL and DC.

Stupid in Other Areas (Ex)

  • Take a penalty to Craft and Spellcraft checks equal to level
  • Take a penalty to any Profession check

True Talent (Ex)

At level 20:

  • Add twice Charisma modifier to caster level
  • Arcane Combining can mix spells of the same level
  • Use Arcane Combining Charisma modifier times per day
