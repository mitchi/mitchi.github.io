Abilities

A Barrier for the Elemental Lord
Igni creates a barrier of fire to protect his elemental lord. The barrier has hp and deals fire damage when it explodes.

Fire Embrace
Igni embraces a target enemy. Fire Embrace deals 4x the damage of Fire Presence and tries to transmute the enemy into a solid fire statue, DC Fortitude save.

Diminish Size
Changes its size at the command of Aldor. Minimum size is medium.

Fire Presence
damage per turn, Radius is equal to Igni’s reach. Fire Presence can be turned off by Aldor with a swift action
